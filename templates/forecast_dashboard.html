Project Quick Explainer (For Presentations)
1. What is the Input? The model uses past groundwater levels and blends them with local weather factors (rainfall, temperature, and humidity) to find seasonal patterns.
2. How does the AI train? It automatically trains multiple AI models (like Random Forest and XGBoost) and picks the one with the lowest prediction error.
3. What is the Output? It forecasts water levels up to 1 year in advance and uses SHAP values to explain which factors (like rainfall or heat) caused the change.
